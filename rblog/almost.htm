While we do support and encourage free speech, our incarnation of RBlog does not allow posting of material that would violate the Canadian Criminal Code. This includes but is not limited to : pornography, hate literature, fraud, illegal activities such as drug use and/or gambling.

Any such posting is done so without our knowledge or permission. Upon learning of such misuse, we will remove content in violation and if need be, cancel the account of the RBlog poster.
